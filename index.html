BRANDS OWNED BY PARAKH




EXAMPANDA

ExamPanda is a brand owned by Parakh Pvt exam Ltd. that provides a complete solution for conducting any type of examinations across India.

BOOKINFRA

Book Infra is developed to give a centralized platform for searching and booking exam venues across India. Book Infra also offers exam venue auditing services to ensure the exam venues meet the examinations requirements. Along with it, Book Infra helps entrepreneurs set up and run their own exam venues.

EDUVUE

EduVue is developed to provide complete institute management solution to the educational institutes i.e. schools, colleges, universities, coaching centers and training centers.

TESTILO

Testilo provides a robust and scalable test engine solution to conduct any type of computer-based, web-based, or tablet-based exams in a secure environment.

HIREHUES

Hire Hues is a human resources solution developed by Parakh Pvt Ltd that provides skilled invigilators, IT infrastructure managers, and other manpower resources to the exam centers for conducting different types of examinations.

LEARNIFY

Learnify is a brand owned by Parakh Pvt. Ltd is providing skill-based and job-based training and certification courses to the individuals and helping them move ahead in their careers.
